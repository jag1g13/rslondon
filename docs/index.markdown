## STEP-UP RSLondon Conference 2026

As part of our partnership with the [STEP-UP project](https://step-up.ac.uk/) RSLondon is co-organising the [STEP-UP RSLondon Conference 2026](https://step-up.ac.uk/events/step-up-2026/). This is the 7th annual conference in the series that was formerly known as RSLondonSouthEast. The conference will take place on **Monday 29th June 2026 at The Francis Crick Institute** in Central London.

Registration for the event closes at midday on Friday 19th June 2026.
