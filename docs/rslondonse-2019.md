## RSLondonSouthEast 2019

#### **Thursday 7th February 2019, 10am – 4.30pm, The Royal Society**

RSLondonSouthEast 2019 will bring together a group of researchers, Research Software Engineers (RSEs), academics and individuals in a variety of related roles who work with or are interested in research software. This is the first of what is planned to be an annual workshop run by the Research Software London and South East community to provide a regional focus on research software activities. Whether you’re already an experienced RSE or researcher developing software or whether you’re new to the area and are keen to develop your skills and meet others working in the field, the workshop will provide a great opportunity to engage with the community, network and develop your research software skills.

The one-day workshop will take place on Thursday 7th February, 10am-4.30pm, in the Dining Room at The Royal Society. There will be a range of presentations from people across the community as well as opportunities for networking and discussion. See the [call for submission](/rslondonse-2019/call-for-submissions/) of abstracts for presentations and posters.
