## Join the mailing list

By subscribing to the list, you are accepting that you will receive regular e-mails from Research Software London. You can unsubscribe from the list at any time via the link sent to you in the details after you subscribe or by following the link to the list information page that is in the footer of every e-mail sent via the list.

The list is managed via Mailman and you can sign up at: [https://mailing-lists.imperial.ac.uk/mailman3/lists/research-software-london.imperial.ac.uk/](https://mailing-lists.imperial.ac.uk/mailman3/lists/research-software-london.imperial.ac.uk/)

### Data Privacy

We are committed to handling your data responsibly. See our [e-mail list/newsletter privacy notice](/rslondon/assets/documents/2018/08/rslondon-mailinglist-privacy-notice-30Aug18.pdf) for details of how data is handled and managed in relation to this e-mail list.

### Further Details

The Research Software London mailing list is used to publicise our events, let you know about community news and information that we think may be of interest our community members and also to forward occasional information from third parties that we think may be of interest. This is not intended as a discussion list and, as such, this is generally a low-traffic e-mail list. E-mails sent to the list may, for example, include:

- Details of our upcoming community events or training courses
- Details of events or courses hosted by local Research Software communities at institutions within our region
- Information from third-parties on events or opportunities that may be of interest to community members

Third parties can send information to the list but e-mails must be approved by a list moderator and will not be forwarded on to list members until they have been approved.

For general Research Software Engineering discussion and Research Software London-specific discussion (on the #rslondon channel) you can [join UK RSE Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/ukrse/signup).
